---FORMATTED OUTPUT---
Format specifier in parantheses
Eg. PRINT '(i5)', j

For INTEGER nix
For CHARACTER nax
For REAL in floating point nfx.y
For REAL in exponential nex.y

n= no, real/integer/character 
x= field width
n=no. of characters reserved for fractional or exponential part

Same principle applies for FORMATTED INPUT.
